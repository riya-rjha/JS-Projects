INTRODUCTION
Alfred Amour Garnier founded the company in Blois, Paris, in 1904, with a primary focus on using natural substances for hair treatment. With time, the company broadened its product line to include skincare and joined a worldwide market. Garnier is adamant about its opinion that nature provides the best self-care solutions. This concept supports Garnier's reputation for offering eco-friendly and successful beauty products, appealing to customers all over the world who value sustainability and efficacy in their skincare routines.

MISSION
With seven main product categories, Garnier has grown from its modest 1904 start to become a well-known body care company worldwide. Garnier is still dedicated to offering premium body care products at reasonable prices, and it offers a wide variety of treatments designed to meet different customer needs. Garnier, who embraces diversity, thinks that everyone should have access to the best self-care possible without going over budget.

GARNIER's Commitment
Garnier is dedicated to reducing its environmental effect in addition to its aesthetic appeal. In keeping with its sustainable philosophy, the company always considers biodegradability while creating product formulae and packaging. Garnier makes environmental responsibility a top priority in order to lessen its environmental impact while still offering high-quality cosmetic products.
